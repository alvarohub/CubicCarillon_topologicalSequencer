Cube Carillon

The cube's faces are a step-sequencer grid. Square reading-heads sweep along two perpendicular groups of tracks, each at its own tempo; when a horizontal and a vertical head meet at an intersection they sound an accent. Drag to rotate; press g for gravity; on a phone, tilt the device.
