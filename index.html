Cube Carillon

The cube's faces are a step-sequencer grid; the glowing pads are armed notes. Square reading-heads sweep along two perpendicular bands — a horizontal head reads pitch from its row, a transversal head from its column, so the same score sounds different rotated 90°. Tap a pad to toggle a note, tap a head to pick its instrument. Press m for a clocked step grid, r to derail, g for gravity; on a phone, tilt.
